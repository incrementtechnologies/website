Since 2017, we have been building solutions

Increment Technologies Inc. is a digital solutions provider that specializes in website and mobile development, as well as IT department outsourcing services. Founded in 2017, our goal has always been to become the leading development firm in Cebu, Philippines. Over the years, we have successfully developed innovative digital products. One of our notable creations is Jiph, formerly known as PayHiram, which is a remittance platform revolutionizing the way Filipinos send money from the US to the Philippines. We are proud to have contributed to facilitating secure and convenient money transfers.


Furthermore, we have created Fitin, a virtual closet that offers personalized recommendations for the best combinations of your clothes. This cutting-edge technology helps users effortlessly enhance their fashion choices. At Increment Technologies Inc., we have established a strong reputation for delivering high-quality digital projects. Our expertise spans from developing simple landing pages to building comprehensive e-commerce websites.


In addition to our web and mobile development services, we now offer IT department outsourcing services, helping businesses achieve their goals through data analytics, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Our team of experts has the knowledge and experience to guide you through the entire process, ensuring that you get the best results possible.


We believe in delivering tailored solutions that meet our clients' specific needs. Whether you're a local startup or a global enterprise, we take the time to understand your requirements and work with you to develop a strategy that aligns with your business goals.


Our commitment to quality has earned us the trust of clients from a wide range of industries, including Fintech, Service delivery, Education (Learning Management System), Hospitality, and E-commerce. We are proud to have served clients from around the world, delivering exceptional results and helping them achieve success. If you're looking for a reliable partner for your web and mobile development needs, or seeking guidance on your IT strategy, look no further than Increment Technologies Inc.
