Jiph: A Remittance App

US, Philippines

Jiph(fka PayHiram) is a cutting-edge mobile remittance platform that aims to transform the way Filipinos send money from the US to the Philippines. By leveraging technology, Jiph provides an innovative and cost-effective solution, revolutionizing the traditional remittance experience.


Say goodbye to high remittance fees and complicated processes. With Jiph, Filipinos can enjoy a faster, more affordable, and incredibly convenient alternative to conventional remittance channels. Our platform streamlines the entire process, ensuring that your hard-earned money reaches your loved ones in the Philippines swiftly and securely.


Experience the future of remittance with Jiph and discover a truly promising solution to the challenges faced by Filipinos when it comes to sending money back home. Join us in revolutionizing the remittance landscape in the Filipino market.

Fitin: A social and personalize apparel e-commerce App

Asia

Fitin wants to make shopping and picking clothes become more effortless and efficient.


Choosing the perfect outfit can be a time-consuming and overwhelming task, especially when people have a special occasion to attend to. This leaves people to be unsure about what to wear daily. Moreover, for some, busy schedules and crowded stores make it even more challenging to find the right clothing style and size, resorting to shopping online which lacks the assurance of product quality and color accuracy without trying the product in person.


This is exactly why Fitin offers a personalized fashion e-commerce that provides outfit recommendations based on your style along with features such as virtual try-on, a closet with a provided avatar, and the option to connect and share their fashion style to a wider audience.
